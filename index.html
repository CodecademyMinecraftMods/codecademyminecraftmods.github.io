Welcome to Atlantis 1.6.4!

This mod is based off of a old, old myth called atlantis, in which there was a lost ancient underwater city that many have attempted to rediscover, and most have failed. The mod is most definently still a W.I.P (work in progress), but that can't stop you from supporting us on Minecraft Forum, and if you are a developer that would like to help, the also on GitHub.
